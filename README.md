# HealthWallet-AU
Open-source platform helping Australians find healthcare services with the lowest out-of-pocket cost based on their insurance coverage.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Problem

Healthcare costs in Australia can be confusing.

Different insurers such as Bupa, Medibank, nib, and HCF offer varying rebates and provider networks.

Patients often don't know:
- which clinics accept their insurance
- what their out-of-pocket costs will be
- where bulk-billing services are available

This leads to unexpected expenses and difficulty accessing affordable healthcare.

---

## Solution

HealthCost Navigator AU helps users:

1. Select their health insurance provider
2. Search for healthcare services (GP, physiotherapy, dental, imaging, etc.)
3. View nearby clinics
4. Estimate out-of-pocket costs

The platform ranks clinics based on affordability and insurance compatibility.

---

## Target Users

- International students
- Migrants
- Temporary visa holders
- Residents with private health insurance
- Anyone seeking affordable healthcare options

---

## MVP Features

- Search healthcare services
- Select insurance provider
- View clinics with estimated out-of-pocket cost
- Identify bulk-billing providers
- Location filtering (Melbourne first)
